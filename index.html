Local Places

Gundu

Gundu is a local and popular picnic spot and scenario spot, where the crashed army plan is one of the main highlight

Nagarkot

Nagarkot is perfect place for sight seeing and enjoying local and premium hospitality

Dhulikhel

Dhulikhel is known for its beautiful landscapes and cultural heritage, making it a must-visit destination for travelers.

Chitlang

Chitlang offers a serene escape with its lush greenery, traditional villages, and panoramic views of the Himalayas.

Panauti

Panauti is a historic town known for its ancient temples, vibrant festivals, and rich cultural heritage.

Bhaktapur

Bhaktapur is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, famous for its well-preserved medieval architecture, art, and culture.

Namobuddha

Namobuddha is a significant Buddhist pilgrimage site, renowned for its tranquil environment and stunning views.

Dhading

Dhading is known for its diverse landscapes, from lush forests to rugged mountains, making it ideal for trekking and nature exploration.

Ilam

Ilam is famous for its tea gardens, rolling hills, and pleasant climate, attracting visitors seeking relaxation and natural beauty.

Palpa

Palpa is known for its historical significance, traditional architecture, and scenic beauty, including views of the Annapurna mountain range.
