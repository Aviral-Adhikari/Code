Local Places

Gundu

Gundu is a local and popular picnic and scenic spot, where the crashed army plane is one of the main highlights.

Nagarkot

Nagarkot is a perfect place for sightseeing and enjoying local and premium hospitality.

Dhulikhel

Dhulikhel is known for its beautiful landscapes and cultural heritage, making it a must-visit destination.

Chitlang

Chitlang offers a serene escape with lush greenery, traditional villages, and panoramic Himalayan views.

Panauti

Panauti is a historic town known for ancient temples, vibrant festivals, and rich cultural heritage.

Bhaktapur

Bhaktapur is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, famous for preserved medieval architecture, art, and culture.

Namobuddha

Namobuddha is a major Buddhist pilgrimage site, known for its tranquility and scenic beauty.

Dhading

Dhading features diverse landscapes, from forests to mountains, ideal for trekking and exploration.

Ilam

Ilam is famous for tea gardens, rolling hills, and a pleasant climate.

Palpa

Palpa is known for historical significance, traditional architecture, and scenic mountain views.
